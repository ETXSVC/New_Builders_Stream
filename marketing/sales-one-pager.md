# Builders Stream

## Construction work moves faster when everyone works from the same picture.

Builders Stream gives growing construction and renovation companies a connected place to manage the work that happens before, during, and around every project.

## The challenge

When leads, estimates, schedules, documents, and jobsite updates are scattered across separate tools, teams lose time and visibility. Important details get buried, handoffs become manual, and project status is harder to trust.

## The solution

Builders Stream connects the core workflow in one practical platform:

- **CRM:** Capture leads, track pipeline status, and log customer communications.
- **Project management:** Create projects, organize phases, assign tasks, and monitor progress.
- **Project records:** Store and version documents where the project team can find them.
- **Field coordination:** Support daily logs and assigned-work visibility for field crews.
- **Estimation and approval:** Create a clearer path from estimate to signed customer approval.
- **Role-based access:** Give admins, managers, crews, accountants, and clients the appropriate view of the work.

## What this means for your team

- Less duplicate entry and fewer manual handoffs
- Faster access to the latest project information
- More consistent follow-through from lead to project kickoff
- Better coordination between office staff and the jobsite
- A clearer, more accountable operating rhythm as the company grows

## Built for the next stage of growth

Builders Stream is designed for companies that need more structure than spreadsheets can provide, without taking on the complexity of an enterprise platform. Start with the workflow that keeps work moving today, with room for future billing, accounting, and integration capabilities.
