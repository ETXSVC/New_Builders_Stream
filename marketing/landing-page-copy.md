## FAQ

### Who is Builders Stream for?

Builders Stream is designed for small and mid-sized construction, renovation, and specialty contracting teams.

### What does Builders Stream help manage?

The product’s MVP focuses on company administration, CRM, project management, document organization, daily logs, estimating, and e-signature approvals.

### Is this accounting software?

Accounting and billing integrations are planned for later stages. Builders Stream’s current focus is the connected operational workflow from lead through project delivery.

### Can field crews use it?

Yes. The platform is designed to give field crews access to assigned project work and daily-log reporting while keeping permissions appropriate to their role.
